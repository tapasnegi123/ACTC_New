About ACTC

The Annual Calendar for Training & Competitions (ACTC) is being created for the various High Priority Disciplines, such as Shooting, Hockey, Wrestling etc. and Priority Order Disciplines such as Cycling, Table-Tennis etc. and several other sports such as the Para-Sports, Kabaddi etc., wherein details regarding National Coaching Camps, National Championships, International Competitions/ Exposures, Coaches, equipment etc. for the entire year is drawn. Short term & long term Milestones for the disciplines are set by National Sports Federations (NSF) in the ACTC. And the ACTC is funded by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports under the Assistance to NSF Scheme, and monitored by the Sports Authority of India.
